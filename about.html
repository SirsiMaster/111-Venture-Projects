The Origin Story

In 2023, our founder lost his father unexpectedly. In the midst of profound grief, he was handed a stack of paperwork three inches thick. The world didn't stop. Bills needed to be paid, the funeral home needed decisions, and the government demanded forms.

There was no guide. No manual. Just a fragmented system of lawyers, banks, and agencies that didn't talk to each other.

Legacy was born from that frustration. We believe that grieving families deserve a shepherd, not a checklist. We use technology to handle the logistics so you can focus on the legacy your loved one left behind.
