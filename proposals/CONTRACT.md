# MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT

**This Master Services Agreement** ("Agreement") is entered into as of December 11, 2025 ("Effective Date"), by and between:

**Client:** FinalWishes Inc. ("Client") and
**Provider:** 111 Venture Studio ("Provider").

## 1. SERVICES
Provider agrees to perform the services described in the Statement of Work ("SOW") and Cost Proposal attached hereto (collectively, the "Services").

## 2. COMPENSATION
Client agrees to pay Provider a total fixed fee of **$95,000 USD** for the Services, payable according to the schedule below:
*   **25% ($23,750)** upon execution of this Agreement ("Kickoff").
*   **25% ($23,750)** upon Alpha Release (Week 8).
*   **25% ($23,750)** upon Beta Release (Week 16).
*   **25% ($23,750)** upon Production Launch (Week 20).

## 3. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
**3.1 Client Ownership.** Upon full payment, Provider assigns to Client all right, title, and interest in and to the custom software developed under this Agreement ("Deliverables").
**3.2 Provider IP.** Provider retains all rights to its background technology, pre-existing code libraries (e.g., "Sirsi"), and development tools used to create the Deliverables. Client is granted a perpetual, non-exclusive, royalty-free license to use such background technology as incorporated into the Deliverables.

## 4. CONFIDENTIALITY
Each party agrees to keep confidential all non-public information disclosed by the other party. Provider acknowledges that it may have access to Client's sensitive business information and agrees to hold such information in strict confidence.

## 5. WARRANTIES
Provider warrants that:
(a) The Services will be performed in a professional and workmanlike manner.
(b) The Deliverables will not infringe upon the intellectual property rights of any third party.
(c) For a period of **60 days** following acceptance (the "Warranty Period"), the Deliverables will substantially conform to the specifications in the SOW.

## 6. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate this Agreement if the other party materially breaches a material term and fails to cure such breach within 15 days of written notice. Upon termination, Client shall pay Provider for all Services performed up to the effective date of termination.

## 7. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Delaware.
