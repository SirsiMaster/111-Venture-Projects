# Communication Plan
## Legacy - The Estate Operating System
**Version:** 1.0.0 | **Date:** November 26, 2025

---

## 1. Stakeholder Communication

| Stakeholder | Method | Frequency | Owner |
|-------------|--------|-----------|-------|
| Project Sponsor | Executive Summary | Weekly (Friday) | PM |
| Steering Committee | Status Meeting | Bi-weekly | PM |
| Development Team | Daily Standup | Daily | Tech Lead |
| Client | Sprint Demo | Bi-weekly | PO |
| Legal Counsel | Email Updates | As needed | PM |

## 2. Meeting Schedule

| Meeting | Day | Time | Duration |
|---------|-----|------|----------|
| Daily Standup | Mon-Fri | 9:30 AM | 15 min |
| Sprint Planning | Day 1 | 10:00 AM | 2 hours |
| Backlog Grooming | Day 5 | 2:00 PM | 1 hour |
| Sprint Review | Day 10 | 2:00 PM | 1 hour |
| Retrospective | Day 10 | 3:30 PM | 1 hour |

## 3. Escalation Path

```
Level 1: Team Lead (< 4 hours)
    ↓
Level 2: Tech Lead / PM (< 8 hours)
    ↓
Level 3: Steering Committee (< 24 hours)
    ↓
Level 4: Project Sponsor (immediate)
```

## 4. Communication Channels

| Purpose | Channel |
|---------|---------|
| Daily communication | Slack #legacy-dev |
| Documentation | Notion / Confluence |
| Code discussions | GitHub PRs |
| Video meetings | Zoom / Google Meet |
| Escalations | Direct message + call |

## 5. Status Reporting

### Weekly Report Contents
- Progress vs. plan
- Key accomplishments
- Blockers & risks
- Next week's priorities
- Budget status
